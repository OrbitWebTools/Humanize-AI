Privacy Policy for OrbitWebTools

Last updated: December 2025

At OrbitWebTools, accessible from https://orbitwebtools.github.io/humanize-ai-text/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors.

Data Processing

We do not store any text you input into our Humanizer tool. All processing happens locally in your browser (Client-Side) via JavaScript. Your essays and content never leave your device.

Cookies

We do not use cookies to track your personal information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, you can contact us via our GitHub repository.
